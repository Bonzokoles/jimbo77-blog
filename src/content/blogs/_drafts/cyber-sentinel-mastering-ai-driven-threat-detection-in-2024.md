## Key Components of AI-Driven Threat Detection Systems  

### 1. **Machine Learning Algorithms**

- Unsupervised learning for detecting novel attack patterns.

### 2. **Natural Language Processing (NLP)**  
- Analyze phishing emails, social media threats, and dark web chatter.  
- Identify malicious intent in unstructured data.  

### 3. **Behavioral Analytics**  
- Establish baseline user behavior to flag anomalies.  
- Detect insider threats and compromised accounts.

- Immediate containment of detected threats.
